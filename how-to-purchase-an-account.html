How To Purchase An Account



Purchasing a trading account with Icon Trading Capital is a straightforward process. We offer several convenient payment methods to facilitate your purchase. Follow these steps to acquire your funded trading account:


1. Choose Your Account Type

First, decide on the type of trading account that best suits your trading goals and requirements. We offer a variety of account options to cater to different trading styles and levels.


2. Select a Payment Method

We provide several payment options for your convenience:

  • Yoco: Use Yoco to make your payment easily and securely. Simply follow the prompts to complete your transaction.
  • PayPal: Pay using your PayPal account for a secure and efficient payment process. Login to your PayPal account and authorize the payment.
  • Cryptocurrency: We accept various cryptocurrencies. Choose your preferred cryptocurrency and follow the instructions to complete the payment.

3. Complete the Purchase

Once you’ve selected your account type and payment method, proceed to complete the purchase. You will receive a confirmation email with details of your purchase and instructions on how to access your funded trading account.


4. Verify Your Account

After completing the payment, verify your account details by following the instructions provided in the confirmation email. Ensure all required information is accurate to avoid any delays.


5. Start Trading

With your account successfully purchased and verified, you can start trading. Make sure to review our trading guidelines and leverage the resources available to maximize your trading success.


If you have any questions or need assistance with your purchase, please contact our support team. We’re here to help ensure a smooth and efficient process.
