How to Request Payout from Icon Trading Capital Prop Firm



If you're looking to request a payout from Icon Trading Capital Prop Firm, follow these steps to ensure a smooth and efficient process:


1. Review Your Trading Account

Before requesting a payout, ensure that your trading account meets all the necessary criteria. This includes having a minimum balance or meeting trading requirements as specified by Icon Trading Capital.

2. Prepare Your Documents

Gather any required documentation, such as proof of identity or bank account details. This is often necessary to verify your request and ensure that payouts are processed to the correct account.


3. Access the Payout Request Form

Log in to your Icon Trading Capital account and navigate to the payouts or withdrawal section. Look for the payout request form. This form is typically found under the 'Account' or 'Funds' menu.


4. Fill Out the Form

Complete the payout request form with the necessary details, including the amount you wish to withdraw and your payment method. Ensure all information is accurate to avoid delays.


5. Submit the Request

Once you have filled out the form, submit your request. You should receive a confirmation email or notification indicating that your request has been received and is being processed.


6. Wait for Processing

Processing times can vary. Typically, payouts are processed within a few business days. Check your account or email for updates on the status of your request.


7. Verify Receipt

Once your payout is processed, verify that the funds have been received in your bank account or chosen payment method. If you encounter any issues, contact Icon Trading Capital’s support team for assistance.


If you have any questions or need further assistance, refer to Icon Trading Capital’s help resources or reach out to their customer support team. Ensuring you follow these steps will help you navigate the payout process smoothly.
