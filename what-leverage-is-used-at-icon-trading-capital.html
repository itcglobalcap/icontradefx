What is the Leverage Used at Icon Trading Capital?



At Icon Trading Capital, leverage plays a crucial role in amplifying your trading potential. We offer different leverage options depending on the type of account you hold. Understanding the leverage associated with your account can help you manage your trades more effectively and optimize your trading strategy.


1. Leverage for Welcome and Start-Up Accounts

If you hold a Welcome or Start-Up account, you benefit from a leverage of 1:200. This level of leverage allows you to control a larger position with a relatively small amount of capital, providing the opportunity for increased trading flexibility and potential returns.


2. Leverage for Rapid and Stellar Accounts

For those with Rapid or Stellar accounts, the leverage offered is significantly higher at 1:2000. This higher leverage provides even greater control over larger trading positions, but it also requires careful risk management to avoid substantial losses.
